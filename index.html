Movie Facts

Did you know?

In 1939, "Gone with the Wind" became the first movie to win more than five Academy Awards.

The first film with a $100 million budget was "True Lies" in 1994.

The highest-grossing movie of all time is "Avengers: Endgame" (2019), with a worldwide box office revenue of $2.798 billion.

The first color film was invented in 1902 by Edward Raymond Turner.

The first full-length animated film was "Snow White and the Seven Dwarfs" (1937).

The first movie ever made was "Roundhay Garden Scene" in 1888, which lasts just over 2 seconds.
